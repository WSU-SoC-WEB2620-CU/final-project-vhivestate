About

As a company, we strive to be a creative and professional studio that values their clients and allows their artists to create quality artwork. Our drive and focus is to deliver only the highest level of art for our treasured clients while also striving for excellent customer service.
Studio Information
  • A minimum one hour at the artists rate deposit is required for any appointment; more for larger appointments.
  • Deposits are applied at the last appointment, or second to last depending on the remaining amount due.
  • You are allowed one re-schedule per piece as long as 7 business days notice is given. If you reschedule more than once your deposit is canceled and a new one must be made.
  • All deposits are NON-REFUNDABLE.
Deposits are forfeited if you cancel or change your appointment within less than 7 business days notice and an additional deposit must be made to reschedule.
