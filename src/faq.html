FAQs

Frequently Asked Questions

Yes, we recommend making an appointment rather than walking into our tattoo shop to ensure that one of our artists is available to work with you.

You must be at least 18 years old to get a tattoo without parental consent. Please bring a valid ID to your tattoo appointment to prove your age.

Yes, we require a deposit to book an appointment. The deposit is non-refundable and will be applied to the cost of your tattoo.

Yes, cover-up tattoos are a common request. Our artists will work with you to design a tattoo that will cover up your existing tattoo.

Our tattoo shop accepts cash, venmo, and major credit cards. Payment is typically made at the end of each session or as agreed upon with your artist.

Many people like to refer that getting a tattoo feels like someone is scratching a hot needle across your skin or others like to compare it to a constant feeling of a cat scratch. After about 15 minutes, your adrenaline will start kick in and you will start to get used to the feeling.

We will provide you with detailed aftercare instructions tailored to your tattoo. Generally, use a bandage system that is worn for a total of 5 days. Depending on the need present, we sometimes recommend aftercare products.

Our tattoo shop does not offer tattoo removal, but we do offer tattoo cover-ups.

As a precaution, we do not tattoo pregnant or breastfeeding individuals.
